The Legal Practice Area

A legal practice area refers to a specific area or field of law in which an
attorney specializes and provides legal services

Business Law

Contracts,torts,intellectual property,antitrust,and business structures are key aspects of business law,guiding legal actions and operations.

Education Law

Education law governs educational systems, policies, funding, rights, responsibilities, and regulations within educational institutions and systems.

Criminal Law

Criminal law concerns crimes, punishments, and legal processes governing offenses against society, defining criminal acts and their consequences"

Child Support

Child support ensures financial assistance for a child's well-being, covering basic needs like food, housing, education, and healthcare."

Divorce Law

Divorce law governs the legal process and issues related to the dissolution of marriage, including property division, alimony, and child custody.

Tax Law

Tax law involves rules and regulations set by government for taxing income, transactions, property, and other financial activities within a jurisdiction
